About Me

I was born in Guayama, Puerto Rico and have been raised there and here in Florida. I've always had a love for art, especially since my mom strived to be an artist. I have a specialty in traditional work like graphite and charcoal drawings, although not much will be shown here.

I am an artist who wants to try every single of art type at least once.
This is meant to be portfolio of sorts to showcase my works as they better and grow.
